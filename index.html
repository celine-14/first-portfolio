Voces Creo

Based in Dublin, Ireland, the Voces Creo is founded in 2020. Bringing together singers from all walks of life, the choir performs choral music of different cultures and across all historical period. The choir is constantly exploring repertoire that are not written by dead white men to promote diversity and inclusion for composers from underrepresented groups.
